Plant care

💧 Watering

Water your plants regularly, but do not overwater. Most flowers need water 2-3 times a week.

🌞 Sunlight

Some flowers need full sun (6+ hours), while others grow better in shade.

🌱 Soil & Fertilizers

Use good soil with nutrients. Fertilize your plants once a month.
